🤍 Coping Tools

You're Not Alone 🤍

When difficult emotions arise, these tools can help you regulate, ground yourself, and find relief. Remember: it's okay to not be okay, and seeking support is a sign of strength.

🫁
Breathing Exercise
Guided breathing techniques to calm your nervous system and reduce anxiety.
🌍
5 Senses Grounding
Identify 5 things you see, 4 you hear, 3 you feel, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.
💭
Thought Challenging
Learn to identify and reframe unhelpful thought patterns.
🎯
Progressive Muscle Relax
Reduce physical tension by progressively relaxing muscle groups.
📝
Worry Dump
Write down everything worrying you to get it out of your head.
💪
Coping Statements
Personalized affirmations to remind yourself of your strength and resilience.

🛤️ Coping Pathways

Sit With The Feeling
For when you want to process and understand your emotions:
  • Acknowledge the feeling
  • Notice where you feel it
  • Journal about it
  • Practice self-compassion
  • Breathe through it
Distract & Reset
For when you need to step away and reset:
  • Engage in an activity
  • Take a walk outside
  • Chat with someone
  • Practice a hobby
  • Come back refreshed
Seek Support
For when you need help beyond self-care:
  • Reach out to a friend
  • Call a loved one
  • Join support community
  • Contact a professional
  • Crisis resources available
